Who may register?

All citizens of the Philippines who:

  • Are at least eighteen (18) years old on election day;
  • Have resided in the Philippines for at least one (1) year.

All voters must have resided at the where he/she intends to vote for at least 6 months before the election day.

Requirements for first time registrants:

  • Bring any valid ID and proceed to the nearest Office of the Election Officer (OEO) at the COMELEC Office.
    •     List of valid IDs:
      •   Postal ID;
      •   PWD Discount ID;
      •   Student’s ID or Library card;
      •   Senior Citizen’s ID;
      •   Driver’s License;
      •   NBI Clearance;
      •   Philippine Passport;
      •   IBP ID;
      •   PRC License;
      •   NCIP Certificate of Confirmation; 
      •   SSS/GSIS ID or other UMID card;
      •   Barangay Identification/Certification with photo;
      •   National identification (ID) card under PhilSys;
      •   Any other Government-issued valid ID
  • Print and fill up this document or you may ask for a copy of it from the election officer: Application Form

             Note: Ensure that the valid ID clearly states your place of residence. Otherwise, you may be asked for other supporting documents stating where you live.

Disqualifications for first time registrants:

  • Non-Filipino citizens;
  • Filipinos under 18 years old;
  • Failure to meet residency requirements; and
  • Legal disqualifications, such as insanity or incompetence, crimes against the state, serious crimes (convictions over 18 months of imprisonment; voting rights shall be regained after serving the sentence).
